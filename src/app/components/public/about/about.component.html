Project Terra

Terra is beginning and we want to welcome you to a project born from our passion for Lineage II and the community that surrounds it. We know that many private servers of this game have a short lifespan, closing within a few months and leaving players with a feeling of loss and disappointment. That's why, at Terra, our commitment is different.

We want to create a stable and lasting server, where players feel that their progress is valued and where decisions are made with the long-term experience in mind. Our commitment is that the server will be available for at least 2 years after the beta phase ends. If the community desires it and the server is sustainable, we are open to extending its duration.

At Terra, we value our players' opinions. We want you to be an active part of this project, where we will implement changes and improvements based on the needs and desires of our community. This is a living project, and with your help, we are convinced that we can create something truly special.
